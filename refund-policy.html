Refund Policy

Refund And Replacement Rules

Effective date: April 9, 2026

Eligibility Window

Refund requests can be submitted within 7 days from the purchase date when files are corrupted, duplicated, or incorrectly delivered.

Non-Refundable Cases

  • Completed downloads with no file issue reported.
  • Accidental purchases after multiple successful downloads.
  • Requests made after the 7-day eligibility window.

How To Request Refund

  • Email support@rocoraft.example with order ID and issue details.
  • Attach screenshot or error proof, if available.
  • Our team reviews and responds within 2 business days.

Return Policy For Digital Goods

As Rocoraft products are digital ebooks, physical returns do not apply. Eligible issues are handled via replacement or refund.
